Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 May, 2023
At Ferret Studios, we take your privacy very seriously. We understand that your personal information is sensitive and private, and we want to assure you that we will never collect or store any information about you or your gameplay.
We do not use any tracking cookies or analytics software that might monitor your online activity. We will not collect your IP address or any other personal identifiable information about you while you play our games.
When you play our games, we do not ask you for any personal information, such as your name, email address, or other contact details. You are free to play our games without providing any personal information.
We will not share any information that we do not collect. We will not sell, rent, or lease any information about you to third parties. We believe that privacy is a fundamental human right, and we are committed to upholding that right for our players.
Please note that some third-party services, such as app stores, may collect certain information about you when you download or use our games. We have no control over these third-party services and their privacy policies. Please review their privacy policies if you have any concerns.
At Ferret Studios, we are committed to providing you with fun and entertaining games without compromising your privacy. If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us at privacy@ferretstudios.com.
Thank you for playing our games!
